Projects

Some of the things I've dabbled on.

Human Resource Management App

Description:

An app written in C# and WPF with MahApps Metro UI for a private client. The app has high complexity because of the client's organizational structure and nature. This app takes heavy HR tasks to be automated and developed with Ease-of-Use in mind.

This app is a complete Human Resource Management System Software, packed with features, like:  Modern and Slick Design  Full-featured basic and advanced Human Resource modules and functions  Biometric Attendance Monitoring  Android Companion App for Attendance Tracking and Leave Applications  Complete Reporting  and more.

Dormitory Management App

Description:

Written in C#, WPF with MahApps Metro and the MVVM pattern. This app takes Dormitory Management to a whole new level, it aims to lessen the tasks of the dorm owner on monitoring the tenants by automating everything. This app was also made for a private client.

This app features everything a dorm owner can only wish for:  Clean and simple UI  Dues notification via Email and SMS  Reservations with reservation notification  Biometric Attendance Monitoring Feature  Automated Email and SMS notifications  Straightforward reporting
